Jordan is emerging as one of the most popular countries to visit in the Middle East. The highlight of any trip to Jordan is Petra, the hidden city in the rock. This is an incredible ancient town carved into the rocks in southern Jordan, and it can be explored by foot or on the back of a camel. Another popular attraction in Jordan is the desert valley of Wadi Rum, where you can take a jeep tour around the orange sand dunes and huge granite mountains.
